As our psyches dispense the demons
that are no longer able to hide

Evenly, and equally,
we balance the scales within our minds

Compromise in this life,
to counter extremism of the past

Past and present together,
anything otherwise is a farce

We become transparent,
on our desert island beach

Your hair, and mine, as one in the wind,
a love no thing may breach.
